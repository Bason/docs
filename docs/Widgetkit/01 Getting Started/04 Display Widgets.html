Display Widgets

Shortcode

The simpliest way to use widgets on your site is to use the shortcodes in your content provided by Widgetkit. Just copy and paste them in your articles and you're ready to go.

Module (Joomla) / Widget (Wordpress)

You can also use in Joomla the Widgetkit Module on any position or in Wordpress the Widgetkit Widget to add Widgets to any sidebar. Just add a Widgetkit Module or a Widgetkit Widget and choose the widget you want to show.
