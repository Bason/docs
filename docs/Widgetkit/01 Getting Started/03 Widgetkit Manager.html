Widgetkit Manger

Widgetkit has a simple and easy to use interface. When you open Widgetkit you'll get an overview of all Widgets on the tapbar. Just click through them to select you needed widget.

Widgetkit has two types of Widgets:

Global effects

These type of widgets like the lightbox or mediaplayer are global widgets that process and scan the complete page. That's why you'll find a settings page where you can define the selector on which elements the effect should take action and of course the effect specifc settings.

Content Widgets

Content type widgets like slidshow, accordion etc. helps you to organize your content in a slick way. You can than include them as shortcodes or modules everywhere on your page even multiple times on the same page.

In the backend you get a list of all added widgets and all required actions to manage them.
